Skills and Jobs

AI Professionals/Engineer

Position Description

An Artificial Intelligence Engineer is an individual who works with traditional machine learning techniques and uses them to create and build models that power AI-based applications. Techniques such as natural language processing and neural networks are heavily utilized and other skills regarding software development are used in tandem to create an AI software/program. They are primarily responsible for using various programming algorithms to build, test and deploy AI models, which are further applicated to other programs as a feature or as a core function. Furthermore, they are also responsible for developing and programming complex networks of algorithms that make up the rudimentary basis of AI, allowing the program to function and behave like a human brain.

Essential Skills

As the field of Artificial Intelligence is relatively new, there are many pathways and skills an individual can take to become an AI developer. As a minimum, you’ll most likely have to meet certain educational requirements. Those requirements include a bachelor’s degree in one of the related fields below:

  • Computer science
  • Data science
  • Information Technology
  • Statistics
  • Finance

Not only that, but certain key skills, soft and technical skills need to be developed and perfected in order to become a successful AI developer. Skills such as:

Complex Understanding of statistics, probability, and linear algebra

As AI models are built using algorithms, they are heavily reliant on statistic, algebra, and calculus. Furthermore, you must be familiarized with probability to interact with the common AI machine learning models, including the “Hidden Markov”, “Gaussian mixture” and “Naïve Bayes” models.

Programming Skills

As with any specialised field of IT, you will need to have a solid and rudimentary understanding of well-known and studied programming languages like C++, Java, R and Python. AI development is not any different. To develop and deploy AI models, you will need to utilize these programming languages.

Soft Skills

    • Communication Skills

    Artificial Intelligence developers are often required to communicate their plans, progress, data and any technical information to a wide range of individuals; whether it be other developers or maybe any possible clients who are interested in the field and technology to be used for the personal or organizational gains. For this reason, you will need to be an excellent verbal communicator.

    • Collaboration Skills

    In tandem with communicational skills, developers in this field often and need to work within a team of other IT developers or with another team member, so you must possess the capability to work efficiently and effectively within a team.

    • Critical Thinking Skills

    To be highly successful within the field of AI, you will need to be able to come up with innovative and exciting AI models or solutions. To achieve this, you must be capable to do some rapid prototyping. As ideas are not necessarily perfected on the first attempt, you will need to be capable of several tests and modifications of models to suit the needs of whoever is interested in your project. Success will fully depend on how useful the model is to a certain client or a company.

    • Creativity

    As the AI field in the IT industry is relatively new, modern, and exciting innovations are being thought of and brought to fruition every day to solve a really complicated problem or to make current technologies easier to use and understand. AI engineers are always on the lookout for tasks that are inefficiently done by humans and create machines that could do better.

Chess Programmer

Position Description

Chess programming, under the broader category of programming, is a specialised role within the IT field to compose and fabricate machines and algorithms that are provided via databases and extensive user data, to compile statistics and best-move-predictions within a given chess position. These programmers are tasked with gathering all necessary code and databases to create the best possible outcome of a position via an extensive min-max algorithm with proper alpha-beta pruning and database searching abilities, as well as managing their coded engine to function as optimally as possible. After all these steps are checked and readied, the chess programmer handles outputting the best possible move (in the given scenario and limits) to emulate either the statistically best move, or the most human-like one in this case.

Essential Skills

As the IT and digital field are new in terms of career opportunities, the sudden resurgence and exponential growth and development of the modern world has provided countless opportunities and jobs for emerging creators and entrepreneurs. Because of how new the concept of coding as a career is, it has slightly different skill requirements as compared to the older more traditional jobs, and these include:

A minimum of a bachelor’s degree in the relevant field(s) of:

  • Information Technology
  • Computer science
  • Software Engineering
  • Any relevant degrees that work closely with the digital industry

Extensive understanding with several coding languages, as well as existing ones within one of the many major chess websites (Lichess with Scala, stockfish with C++ and Javascript).

Programming Skills

As a chess programmer, these are necessary aspects of the jobs as they work together in tandem to both manage a chess engine within the site(s), as well as having an existing degree with necessary qualifications in order to work at an optimum level without distributing much needed company resources with internships and apprenticeships for a certain employee(s).

These coding languages are crucial to the function of chess engines as they serve as the foundation for all algorithms to function at. For example, stockfish is developed with the help of C++, and thus having prior (extensive) knowledge and/or background with this language would prove useful to help contribute towards the source code, as well as creating new concepts and implementations within the given labels.

Because chess programmers specialise in the fields of creating chess engines and their necessary information, a good grasp of collating necessary data, as well as thinking critically under pressure in important time-limited situations, as well as adapting to the environment.

Soft Skills

    • Proper Communication

    Proper coding integration is an important aspect of creating an efficient chess energy. However, this is impossible to achieve if a team has no cohesion with a given project, the whole project can stagnate or even crumble. To offset this, a chess programmer must be very tightly interwoven with his team and communicate with them as properly and frequently as possible, to ensure updates are to be relayed to the whole group and its members.

    • Efficient problem solving

    The IT field is a complete maze of problem solving on the spot, adapting with the flow of work and situation, whilst remaining calm and rational throughout. Interpreting this and thinking in an abstract manner allows chess programmers to innovate through complex problems and to imagine wild scenarios to hypothetical situations while having the ability to follow through with even wilder solutions.

    • Creativity

    Just like traditional media, programming and coding are forms of art; acting as intricate instructions. Being able to think freely allows a chess programmer to compose wild and even unfeasible solutions that surprisingly work. Having a team that can think freely like this will be extremely beneficial.

    • The ability to scrutinise

    Programming is a fragile art, where one incorrect syntactical error can completely brick the full workings of a written code. Chess programmers must be able to write their code properly and have keen eyes to search through the code and to pick out any glaring errors that might cause issues with running the engine and/or GUI.

Data Analysts

Position Description

Data Analysts job is to simply analyse the data that is provided to them, whether it be data involving numbers, games, statistics of any kind, their job is to solve a certain problem or even find some occurring problems within the data that they receive, gaining insight along the way. In this context, the data analyst would need to determine if the chess engine is appropriately doing its job of adapting to the user’s skill level, and even checking if it’s receiving the necessary data to switch up its way of processing its moves. And after gathering all the data, processing it, checking for some positives and negatives, they are to present it to the people in charge, which in this case is the people who made the engine.

Essential Skills

There are both Soft and career specific skills needed for this job, especially when it involves programming in some form and in a language that the analyst is familiar with. But before that, there are some education qualifications required:

The person would need to receive a bachelor’s degree in either:

  • Data Analytics

  • Any degree involving analysis of data

Machine Learning Engineer

Position Description

A Machine Learning Engineer is a person who specialises in Artificial Intelligence systems by building and designing automated predictive models. They are responsible for creating the algorithms we use today when working with systems that utilise machine learning. These algorithms are capable of making predictions and help in automating workloads. Data scientists, analysts and data engineers typically work alongside machine learning engineers in part of a wider data science team to achieve the goals set out by management. In relation to this current project, a machine learning engineer would work alongside the artificial intelligence engineers to develop the chess engine with an integrated machine learning algorithm. The data analysts dedicated to this project would also work in collaboration with the machine learning engineers to ensure the data needs are being met.

Essential Skills

An undergraduate degree in any of the following applicable fields is critical when starting a career as a machine learning engineer:

  • Computer Science
  • Information Technology
  • Computer Programming
  • Mathematics
  • Data Science

An undergraduate degree may not be enough to start a career in this field and a master’s degree in any of the above would be seen as highly advantageous. A combination of soft and technical skills related to machine learning is essential.

Programming Skills

Being proficient in many programming languages is essential as a machine learning engineer will be utilizing these to code the various algorithms needed. Being familiar with at least one of the following languages; Python, R, Java, SQL is vital.

Computer Science Fundamentals

Working with complex algorithms, machine learning engineers need to use their computer science fundamental skills to search, sort and perfect their work. They need to understand data structures such as stacks, queues, trees and graphs.

Applying Machine Learning Algorithms

An in-depth knowledge of how to apply machine learning algorithms and choosing the right techniques and learning methods is a much-needed skill. Machine learning APIs, packages and libraries such as Spark MLib, TensorFlow, Theano, Keras, H2O are widely available but being able to implement and understand them is necessary as a machine learning engineer.

Soft Skills

    • Attention to Detail

    As with most jobs in this field, attention to detail plays a huge role in landing a successful position as a machine learning engineer. Being able to debug programs and spot any errors in your code is essential. Finding and fixing mistakes will ensure the chess engine runs smoothly and free of any errors.

    • Communication

    Machine learning engineers must have excellent written and verbal communication skills as they will be working in a wider team to complete projects. With our adaptive chess engine project, the machine learning engineers will need to work alongside each and every team member on the project to update them on their progress and present their developments.
